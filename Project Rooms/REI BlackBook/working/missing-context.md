# Missing Context

Needed before drafting or publishing:

- Website purpose and primary audience.
- Desired pages and navigation order.
- Primary call to action.
- Business phone number, email address, form routing, and appointment-routing preference.
- Approved logo, colors, photos, testimonials, and brand language.
- Compliance or disclaimer language for real-estate, investment, financing, rent-to-own, or seller-financing claims.
- Whether to create a new REI BlackBook site or update an existing one.
- Whether the browser is already logged in to the correct REI BlackBook account.
- Publishing approval, domain choice, and DNS-change authority.
- Which REI BlackBook area Wes wants mapped next in detail: Smart Contacts, Deals/Pipelines, Marketing Workflows/Callflows, Properties, ListHQ, or a specific existing website.
- For any live-site update: the exact target site, target page, content change, whether to save as draft or publish, and whether forms/workflows should be tested.
- For the Gracious Millionaire site: approved public page copy, page list, book excerpt text, opt-in consent language, excerpt delivery method, recipient notification routing, and whether to connect a custom domain.
