# Missing Context

## Transaction Facts Needed

- Exact seller name and legal type for the transaction: individual, estate, trust, LLC, corporation, or other entity.
- Whether the property was constructed by the seller.
- Whether the seller has provided seller financing for any other property in the same 12-month period.
- Property state and county.
- Whether the buyer will occupy the property as a principal dwelling.
- Whether there will be any balloon payment, adjustable rate, prepayment penalty, or negative-amortization feature.
- Whether any simultaneous loan or existing subject-to financing will affect buyer obligations.
- Who will be responsible for taxes, insurance, HOA dues, repairs, utilities, and maintenance.

## Policy Decisions Needed From Wes

- Minimum documentation package for every tenant-buyer.
- Whether to require outside loan-originator or mortgage-compliance review even for one-property transactions.
- Whether to set internal approval thresholds for debt-to-income ratio, residual income, reserves, rental-payment history, and credit-history red flags.
- Whether to reject applications that cannot be verified with third-party records.
- Whether to require housing counseling or legal-review acknowledgement from the buyer.

## Legal Review Needed

- Applicability of Dodd-Frank / Regulation Z seller-financer exclusions to Buy Your Home's actual seller entity.
- State-specific Contract for Deed, foreclosure/forfeiture, disclosure, recording, usury, licensing, fair-lending, and fair-housing requirements.
- Whether the process needs Equal Credit Opportunity Act / Fair Credit Reporting Act notices when buyer applications are declined or conditioned.
