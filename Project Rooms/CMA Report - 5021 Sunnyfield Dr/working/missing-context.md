# Missing Context

## Required Before Final CMA

- Subject year built/manufactured.
- Manufacturer/model if available.
- Square footage.
- Bedrooms and bathrooms.
- Condition, updates, roof, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, flooring, and skirting.
- Whether the sale includes land.
- If leased-lot/home-only: monthly lot rent, park approval requirements, age restrictions, pet restrictions, and transfer fees.
- If land-owned: parcel size, tax value, zoning, septic/well or public utilities, and whether the home is permanently affixed.
- MLS or COMPER closed sales of similar manufactured homes.
- Current photos.

## Prototype Assumptions

- Subject is a manufactured home at 5021 Sunnyfield Dr, Raleigh, NC 27610.
- No assumption is made that the sale includes land.
- No assumption is made that the home is financeable as real property.
