# SOP - REI BlackBook Text Response Workflow

## Purpose

Read incoming REI BlackBook / Profit Dial text messages and prepare safe replies without accidentally sending external messages.

## When To Use

Use this when Boss asks Jean Wright to review text messages in REI BlackBook or when REI text messages need follow-up.

## Owner

- Role: Jean Wright / Office Assistant
- Approval: Boss or Mrs Boss for external replies

## Category

Lead Sourcing / Communications

## Tools / Systems

- REI BlackBook
- Profit Dial
- Codex in-app browser

## Access Notes

- REI BlackBook must be logged in inside the Codex in-app browser.
- A Chrome login session is not visible to Codex unless the same session is opened inside the Codex browser.
- Do not send texts while learning or reviewing unless Boss or Mrs Boss explicitly approves the exact reply.

## How To Open The Text Inbox

1. Log into `https://my.reiblackbook.com/`.
2. Official support-center route: click `Marketing` in the top dashboard menu, then click `Inbox` under Profit Dial.
3. In the left sidebar, click `Texts` under Inbox.
4. Alternative live-dashboard route: open the top notification dropdown, use the speech-bubble/text icon to filter text notifications, then click a text notification.
5. The text inbox URL pattern is:
   - `/profitdial/inbox/texts`
   - or a details URL like `/profitdial/inbox/texts/details/...`
6. The inbox table shows received date/time, contact name, sender phone number, Profit Dial number, message preview, and row actions.

## How To Open A Conversation

1. In the text inbox, click the relevant incoming text row or message preview.
2. REI opens a conversation modal.
3. The modal shows:
   - contact name,
   - contact phone number,
   - selected Profit Dial sending number,
   - prior conversation bubbles,
   - a reply text box at the bottom,
   - a red send/envelope button.

## How To Prepare A Reply

1. Read the full conversation history in the modal.
2. Confirm the selected Profit Dial number is correct.
3. Draft a reply outside REI first, or type only after the exact reply is approved.
4. If the contact has opted out, says STOP, REMOVE, or asks not to be contacted, do not reply with a marketing message.
5. If the message requests a call, appointment, price, legal/tax advice, or negotiation, summarize it for Boss before replying.
6. Official support-center steps say to select the Profit Dial number, type the reply in the text box, optionally use Merge Fields or Landing Pages, then click the envelope icon to send.

## Sending Rule

- Jean Wright may read and summarize REI texts.
- Jean Wright may draft proposed replies.
- Jean Wright must ask Boss or Mrs Boss before sending any external lead/customer text.
- Do not click the red send/envelope button unless the exact outgoing message has been approved, except for safe internal replies to Boss or Mrs Boss under the standing exception below.
- Standing exception: Jean Wright may answer REI / Profit Dial text messages sent to `(919) 899-2310` only when the sender is Boss or Mrs Boss.
- Under this standing exception, respond directly in REI to Boss or Mrs Boss with a concise acknowledgement or action confirmation when safe.
- Boss sender verification: (213) 293-7539.
- Mrs Boss sender verification: (703) 346-1256.
- This standing exception does not apply to any other sender or any other Profit Dial number.

## Compliance Notes

- REI BlackBook's text training says contacts must be opted in before text messages can be sent.
- REI BlackBook's SMS compliance guide says promotional messages generally require express written consent.
- Consumer-initiated conversational messages are treated differently from promotional campaigns, but opt-out requests must still be respected.
- If a contact says STOP, REMOVE, unsubscribe, or otherwise asks not to be contacted, follow the remove-request SOP and do not send a marketing reply.
