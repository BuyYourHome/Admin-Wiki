# SOP - Item 098: Reconnect QuickBooks Connections

## Purpose

Reconnect bank or credit card accounts in QuickBooks when an account shows that it needs attention.

## When To Use

Use this when QuickBooks shows a bank or credit card account needs to be reconnected.

## Owner

- Role: Accounting / Office Assistant
- Backup: Wes or Jenny

## Category

Accounting

## Inputs Needed

- QuickBooks access
- Bank or credit card login from approved credential records
- List of accounts showing `needs attention`

## Tools / Systems

- QuickBooks
- Bank or credit card website
- Approved credential storage or statement binder

## Steps

1. Open QuickBooks.
2. Locate accounts marked `needs attention`.
3. Click the bank or credit card account that needs reconnection.
4. Click Reconnect your account.
5. Log in to the related bank or credit card account using approved credentials.
6. Follow QuickBooks prompts to reconnect the account.
7. Confirm the account no longer shows `needs attention`.
8. Repeat for each disconnected account.
9. Note that Home Depot does not connect to QuickBooks under the current source instructions.
10. If an account cannot reconnect, add it to Questions for Review or escalate to Wes.

## Decision Rules

- Do not store banking passwords in the SOP page.
- Use only approved credential sources.
- Treat Home Depot as a known exception unless later instructions change.

## Common Mistakes

- Reconnecting the wrong bank account.
- Using outdated credentials.
- Assuming every vendor account supports QuickBooks connection.
