# SOP - Item 097: Tracking Project Management Worksheet

## Purpose

Review active project management sheets weekly and follow up on tasks that need attention.

## When To Use

Use this once per week for active rehab/property projects and whenever task completion needs to be updated.

## Owner

- Role: Rehab / Office Assistant
- Backup: Wes or Jenny

## Category

Rehab

## Inputs Needed

- Active project management sheets
- Task Assignment spreadsheet
- Updates from Wes and Jenny
- Related tasks for utilities, Zillow, website, and rehab tracking

## Tools / Systems

- Microsoft Teams
- Project Management worksheets
- Task Assignment spreadsheet

## Steps

1. Open Teams.
2. Go to All Properties.
3. Locate the active project management sheets. They are stored in All Properties, not inside individual property folders.
4. Review each active sheet once per week.
5. Do not alter formulas, linked sheets, or structure unless instructed.
6. Update only task completion status when a task is complete.
7. Review tasks that are due, overdue, or waiting on someone.
8. Communicate with Jenny and Wes about tasks assigned to them.
9. Reference the Task Assignment spreadsheet to confirm who owns each task.
10. When utilities are ready to change into the company name, follow Item 067.
11. Use the company shown at the top of the Profit tab to determine BYH vs. SYH.
12. Confirm whether property-related tasks are complete, including Zillow claiming and website listing when applicable.
13. Record follow-up notes or questions before closing the sheet.

## Decision Rules

- Only completion status should be changed unless Wes approves structural changes.
- Active project management sheets are shared and linked, so edits can affect other sheets.
- Use related SOPs for detailed steps rather than improvising inside the worksheet.

## Common Mistakes

- Editing formulas or linked fields.
- Looking inside individual property folders instead of All Properties.
- Failing to follow up with the assigned person.
