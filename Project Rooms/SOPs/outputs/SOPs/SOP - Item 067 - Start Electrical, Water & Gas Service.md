# SOP - Item 067: Start Electrical, Water, and Gas Service

## Purpose

Start utility service for a property under the correct company account.

## When To Use

Use this during property rehab setup when utilities need to be placed into Buy Your Home LLC or Sell Your Home LLC.

## Owner

- Role: Property Rehab / Office Assistant
- Backup: Wes or Jenny

## Category

Property Rehab

## Inputs Needed

- Property address
- Owning company: Buy Your Home LLC or Sell Your Home LLC
- Company tax ID from approved internal records
- Account contact information from approved internal records
- Which services are needed: water, electric, gas
- Project Management Sheet line 15 and 16

## Tools / Systems

- Project Management Sheet in Teams
- Utility provider websites or phone systems
- Company credential records

## Steps

1. Open the Project Management Sheet for the property in Teams under All Properties > `_Project Management [property address]`.
2. Review lines 15 and 16 for utility setup requirements.
3. Confirm the property address.
4. Confirm whether the company is Buy Your Home LLC or Sell Your Home LLC.
5. Gather the approved company tax ID and account contact information from internal records.
6. Identify which services need to be started.
7. Contact the correct provider for each required service.
8. For water, check whether service is through City of Raleigh Water, Carolina Water Service of North Carolina, or another local provider.
9. For electric, check whether service is through Duke Energy or another electric provider.
10. For gas, check whether service is through Dominion Energy or another gas provider.
11. Start service under the correct company name.
12. Record account numbers, start dates, and confirmation details in the property records.
13. Update the Project Management Sheet to show the task is complete.

## Decision Rules

- Use the company shown at the top of the Profit tab or current project record.
- Do not use personal account information unless Wes specifically approves.
- Store tax IDs and credentials in the approved internal location, not in the SOP text.

## Common Mistakes

- Starting service under the wrong company.
- Missing gas service when only electric and water were checked.
- Forgetting to update the Project Management Sheet after setup.
