# SOP - Item 112: Record Invoice for Timesheet

## Purpose

Create, save, and email a contractor invoice from the weekly timesheet.

## When To Use

Use this when a W-9 contractor needs to submit an invoice for the week.

## Owner

- Role: Contractor / Office Assistant
- Backup: Wes or Jenny

## Category

Accounting

## Inputs Needed

- Contractor timesheet
- Week start and end dates
- Contractor folder in Teams

## Tools / Systems

- Microsoft Teams
- Timesheet workbook
- Email

## Steps

1. Open the contractor timesheet in Teams under Office Admin > W-9 Contractors > contractor folder.
2. Check the dates for the week that needs to be invoiced.
3. Go to the invoice tab.
4. Enter the week starting date.
5. Enter the week ending date.
6. Export the invoice as a PDF.
7. Save the PDF invoice back to the contractor folder in Teams.
8. Email the invoice to Wes.
9. CC Jenny on the email.
10. Confirm the sent email and saved PDF are both present.

## Decision Rules

- Use the dates from the actual timesheet week.
- Save the invoice before sending it.
- CC Jenny on invoice emails unless instructed otherwise.

## Common Mistakes

- Using the wrong weekly date range.
- Exporting the wrong tab.
- Sending the email without saving the PDF to Teams.
