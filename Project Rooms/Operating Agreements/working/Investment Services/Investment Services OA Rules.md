# Investment Services OA Rules

These rules govern Investment Services LLC operating-agreement drafts in `Investment Services` mode.

## Mode

- Mode: `Investment Services`
- Entity: `Investment Services LLC`
- Working folder: `Project Rooms\Operating Agreements\working\Investment Services\`
- Source approach: build from the SYH/Simplified OA framework, with changes similar to BYH but modified for Investment Services.

## Source Rule

- Do not use the Wyoming Investment Services OA as the drafting source unless Wes explicitly changes this rule.
- Treat the Wyoming Investment Services OA as background/reference only.
- Use the SYH/Simplified OA or approved Reassembled OA Check as the drafting source for Investment Services builds.
- Keep Jeff Watson/SYH drafting-source lineage visible.
- Refresh the Simplified OA from Teams/SharePoint before a derivative build when the connector is available, because Wes may edit that file directly in Teams.
- If Teams/SharePoint refresh is unavailable, disclose that limitation in the build notes and final response.

## BYH-Derived Process Rule

Investment Services mode should reuse the BYH process structure, not BYH entity facts.

Reuse from BYH:

- Fresh-build discipline: build from the source, not from the last V-numbered draft, unless Wes names a prior draft as the source.
- Cumulative instruction tracking.
- Version-first filenames.
- Source inventory updates.
- Reference-sensitive editing rules.
- Structural DOCX checks and visual render attempt.
- Preservation of source formatting and source-lineage colors.
- Clean-edit exceptions for certification/signature pages and Exhibit A when historical redline text would confuse signing or ownership records.

Do not reuse from BYH unless separately confirmed for Investment Services:

- Browning Family Revocable Trust as sole Member.
- Trustee voting authority.
- BYH managers.
- BYH S-corp assumptions.
- BYH Exhibit A facts.
- BYH certification/signature block facts.

## Drafting Colors And Lineage

- Preserve Jeff Watson red edits as source edits.
- Preserve Wes simplified blue text as source-origin simplified language.
- Use green font for Investment Services additions and replacement text unless Wes changes the color rule.
- Use strikethrough for SYH/retirement-account language that does not apply to Investment Services, except for approved clean-edit areas.
- Do not delete numbered clauses in review drafts merely because they do not apply. Preserve numbering unless Wes approves a clean final version or renumbering pass.

## Investment Services Facts To Confirm

Do not treat any of these as final until confirmed by Wes and, where needed, attorney/CPA review:

- Current Member or Members.
- Membership percentages or units.
- Manager or Managers.
- Whether Investment Services LLC should be manager-managed, member-managed, or both.
- Tax classification.
- Whether Investment Services LLC serves as a manager for BYH or other entities and whether that role affects this OA.
- Effective date.
- Signature authority and signer titles.
- Exhibit A content.

## Known Starting Assumptions

These are working assumptions only:

- Target entity legal name: `Investment Services LLC`.
- The SYH/Simplified OA framework is the drafting template.
- The Wyoming Investment Services OA should not control the new draft.
- Retirement-account-specific SYH provisions likely require review for applicability, but do not strike them wholesale until the Investment Services ownership/tax facts are confirmed.

## Build Authorization

- Do not create a new Investment Services OA draft merely because Wes gives process corrections, review comments, assumptions, or drafting preferences.
- Record process corrections as rules or open issues.
- Build a new draft only when Wes explicitly says to build, proceed, create the draft, or gives another clear action instruction.

## Reference Safety

- Treat section, article, exhibit, and paragraph-label edits as reference-sensitive.
- Do not renumber sections, articles, exhibits, or paragraph labels unless Wes approves a renumbering or final-clean pass.
- Prefer adding language inside an existing section or using suffix-style numbering instead of shifting later references.
- Before a draft is review-ready, audit internal references affected by the change.

## Attorney / CPA Review

Flag these for review before final use:

- Tax classification and tax-election language.
- Ownership and member-rights provisions.
- Manager authority and signature authority.
- Any retirement-account, prohibited-transaction, ERISA, UBTI, UDFI, or disqualified-person language retained, struck, or modified.
- Any reliance on the Wyoming OA as background.
