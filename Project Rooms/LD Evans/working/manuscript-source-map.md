# Manuscript Source Map

## Current Draft Scope

The current source set supports a first manuscript pass for:

- Chapter 4: Going West.
- Chapter 5: Shepherd.

The videos are labeled Chapters 4 and 5. No source material for Chapters 1 through 3 has been provided, so those chapters are not drafted or reconstructed.

## Narrative Voice

The recordings use:

- first-person recollection;
- plainspoken, conversational sentences;
- frequent memory qualifications, such as uncertainty about age, distance, and timing;
- close technical detail when describing machinery;
- repeated emotional anchors, especially homesickness, fear, gratitude, responsibility, and remorse;
- Christian testimony and moral reflection arising directly from lived events.

The working manuscript preserves these traits while removing caption repetition and tightening oral digressions. It does not represent a verbatim transcript.

## Drafting Boundary

The current manuscript adds pacing, paragraphing, and transitions, but it does not add invented scenes, dates, weather, physical appearances beyond the recordings, or internal thoughts unsupported by the narrator's own stated emotions.
