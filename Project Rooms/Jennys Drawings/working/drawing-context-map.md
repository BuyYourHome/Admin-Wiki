# Drawing-to-Manuscript Context Map

## Proposed Companion

Working title: *Drawn by Grace: A Visual Companion to The Gracious Millionaire*

Form: a source-grounded companion manuscript that follows the major spiritual and practical movements of *Gracious Millionaire* without duplicating its prose. Each selected drawing receives a full-page framed plate immediately after the passage it illuminates.

## Editorial Rules

- Draft from the source-derived *Gracious Millionaire* architecture and original-source boundaries; do not treat a prior manuscript mode's prose as factual authority.
- Preserve unresolved outcomes, especially Rosebrooks and the orphaned property.
- Do not promise wealth, victory, healing, or favor as automatic results of faith.
- Keep Jenny's drawings in Jenny's voice: do not invent explanations of what she meant. Describe only what is visible and use the artwork as a thematic lens.
- Use one selected capture per concept and exclude adjacent duplicates.
- Place each selected drawing on its own full page with a tasteful frame, generous white space, a short caption, scripture reference, and drawing date when legible.
