## Short Summary

The estate document set shows one named business entity directly tied to the trust: **Buy Your Home, LLC**. The assignment document transfers the full membership interest in Buy Your Home, LLC from Wesley Dale Browning and Jeanette Wilson Hollinger to the **Browning Family Revocable Trust dated June 21, 2023**.

The Browning Family Revocable Trust is the owner/holder of that LLC membership interest under the assignment. Wes and Jeanette are the trust grantors and initial co-trustees. After either Wes or Jeanette dies, the survivor acts as trustee alone. If both cease serving, Olivia Ashley Morello and Kendall Meredith Hyatt are named as successor co-trustees, with either able to serve alone if the other cannot.

## Buy Your Home, LLC

The assignment identifies Buy Your Home, LLC as a North Carolina limited liability company.

The assignment states that Wesley Dale Browning and Jeanette Wilson Hollinger were the transferring members and held a combined 100% of the company membership interest, split 50% to Wes and 50% to Jeanette.

The assignment transfers the full combined 100% membership interest to the Browning Family Revocable Trust. It states that, after the transfer:

- Wesley Dale Browning: 0%
- Jeanette W. Hollinger: 0%
- Browning Family Revocable Trust dated June 21, 2023: 100%

The assignment also says the company continues and is not dissolved or wound up because of the transfer.

## Browning Family Revocable Trust

The trust document identifies Wesley Dale Browning and Jeanette Wilson Hollinger as grantors of the Browning Family Revocable Trust.

During the grantors' lifetime, trust income and principal are available to them as the trustee directs, and undistributed income may be added to principal.

The trust gives the trustee broad authority over business interests. The trustee may continue to own, form, and operate business interests, including LLC interests, and may change the form of organization, sell or acquire interests, modify or terminate business agreements, invest or lend money to the business, and exercise business powers even where a fiduciary/individual conflict may exist.

The trust also gives the trustee general investment, sale, management, borrowing, lending, nominee-title, securities-rights, and document-execution powers relevant to holding and operating business or real estate assets.

## Wills And Pour-Over Relationship

Wes's will leaves his residuary estate to Jeanette if she survives him. If Jeanette has predeceased him, Wes's residuary estate passes to the Browning Family Revocable Trust.

Jeanette's will leaves her residuary estate to Wes if he survives her. If Wes has predeceased her, Jeanette's residuary estate passes to the Browning Family Revocable Trust.

This means the wills support the trust as the fallback recipient for residuary estate assets if the spouse does not survive, while the separate LLC assignment already places Buy Your Home, LLC membership interest directly into the trust.
