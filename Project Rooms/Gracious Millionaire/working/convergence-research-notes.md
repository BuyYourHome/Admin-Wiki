# Convergence Research Notes

## Purpose

These notes support the Gracious Millionaire chapter draft about Wes's database/systems career and the idea of convergence.

## Source Summary

- Lance Wallnau describes "career convergence" as the place where personal mastery converges with divine assignment. Source: https://lancewallnau.com/the-mountain-of-me/
- Wallnau also describes career convergence as the overlap of calling and income, where work aligns with calling and passion. Source: https://lancewallnau.com/how-to-ride-the-creative-edge-of-chaos/
- In a promotional page for his life-strategy teaching, Wallnau describes convergence as a destiny "sweet spot," says it involves unpacking life journey from design and history toward convergence, and says the average age for convergence in the Western Hemisphere is after 50, with the 60-to-70 decade often especially powerful. Source: https://lancewallnau.com/your-ultimate-life-strategy-in-2021/
- Wallnau's broader calling framework emphasizes that believers often find calling in ordinary occupations and cultural spheres rather than only inside church work. Source: https://lancewallnau.com/episode-2032-finding-your-calling-origin-story-of-7-mountains/
