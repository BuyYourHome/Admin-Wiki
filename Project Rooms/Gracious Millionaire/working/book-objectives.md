## Working Implications

- Do not let property sequence, profit, or deal mechanics become the whole point of a chapter.
- Use the property story as concrete evidence for a broader life lesson.
- For every chapter draft or revision, ask: what lesson is visible here, and what part of that lesson is still unfinished in Wes?
- Preserve humility. The book should not present Wes as someone who has mastered every lesson he is describing.
