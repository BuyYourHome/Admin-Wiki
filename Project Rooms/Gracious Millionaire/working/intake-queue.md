# Gracious Millionaire Intake Queue

This queue tracks only new or changed substantive source material that still needs manuscript processing. Sent-copy and packaging records belong in `delivery-register.md`, not this queue.

| Received | Source | Source Hash | Intended Mode | Affected Chapter Or Placement | Status | Processed Version | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

## Status Values

- `new`: not yet reviewed.
- `mapped`: placement and affected chapter identified.
- `drafted`: chapter change prepared but not compiled.
- `compiled`: manuscript packet rebuilt and validated.
- `delivered`: required review packet sent and verified.
- `deferred`: source preserved but intentionally not processed yet.
- `blocked`: a specific decision or unavailable dependency prevents processing.
