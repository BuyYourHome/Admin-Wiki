## Source Summary

Jenny describes remarriage several years after moving to North Carolina and after buying the Garden City Beach condo. She believed the relationship would be permanent, but it ended abruptly and left her with raw emptiness, failure, fear, and a loss of confidence in her usual caution. Wanting to escape pain, gossip, reminders, and threats to her safety, she tried to relocate near her South Carolina beach condo.

She spent several winter/off-season days searching for a rental near the coast, with repeated rejections, often because of her seventy-pound poodle. On the final night before returning to Raleigh, her laptop fell and the screen scrambled while she was at the end of herself. She cried out to the Lord; Linda called at 10 p.m., came over, helped restore the laptop with her husband, and stayed to reassure Jenny. The next morning, while Jenny was driving back toward Raleigh, a Pawleys Island management company called with available condos. Jenny turned around, brought the poodle into the office, received keys to view new condos, applied, and was accepted to move in immediately.
