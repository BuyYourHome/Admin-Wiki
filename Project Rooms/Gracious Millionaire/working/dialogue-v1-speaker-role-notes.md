# Dialogue v1 Speaker Role Notes

## Wes

Wes carries first-person memory, spiritual interpretation, business-system lessons, property lessons, favor, assignment, gratitude, and unfinished learning. His voice should remain reflective rather than triumphant.

## Jenny

Jenny carries caution, safety, practical burden, relational reality, emotional honesty, and correction where her own source material supports it. Her chapters remain whole and recognizable rather than being absorbed into Wes's lessons.

## Narrator

The narrator may bridge source gaps, identify contextual records, and mark unanswered questions. The narrator must not make Wes or Jenny say unsupported facts or conclusions.
