## Integration Approach

The sermon is treated as contextual source material, not as controlling autobiographical source. The original Gracious Millionaire chapter sources remain authoritative for Wes and Jenny's story.

The concepts used in manuscript draft v5 are:

- God rewards those who diligently seek Him.
- Expectation should grow from trust in the Father's goodness, not entitlement.
- The parable of the talents frames increase as stewardship, not greed.
- What God has already placed in a person's hand should be stewarded instead of buried.
- Reward belongs inside relationship with God, not outside it.
- Faith-filled expectation has a different language than complaint.
