# Rewrite v27 Whole-Book Thesis Map

## Thesis

*Gracious Millionaire* is not a book about using faith to become wealthy. It is a book about what increase, favor, pressure, systems, risk, marriage, technology, and unfinished outcomes reveal about lordship.

## Through-Lines

| Through-line | Carried by | Complicated by | Resolved or left live |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Money under lordship | Opening, Abundance, Closing | Private money, Providence Landing, Orphaned Property | Left live as a repeated heart test. |
| Jenny's caution as wisdom | Jenny chapters, Orphaned Property, Providence Landing | Wes's systems/vision instinct | Reframed as protection and formative witness, not resistance. |
| Favor as assignment | Favor, Red Quartz, First Classrooms | Failed real estate, Rosebrooks, Providence Landing | Defined as cooperation with assignment rather than guaranteed outcomes. |
| Systems as stewardship | Convergence, Technology, Early Properties | AI speed, operational scale, impatience | Systems are useful only when submitted to purpose and people. |
| Unfinished lessons | Foreword, Leadership, Orphaned Property, Closing | Every chapter's temptation to make a clean maxim | Preserved as visible but not fully mastered. |
| Surrender after pressure | Jenny Coastal Surrender, Providence Landing, Orphaned Property | Desire to fix, force, or control outcomes | Treated as a recurring spiritual discipline. |

## Structural Decision

The rewrite keeps a broad chronological arc but lets themes control emphasis. Jenny's material appears before Wes's later success stories because her history changes how the reader should interpret caution, safety, and peace. Technology appears after Convergence because it is not a separate novelty; it is the latest expression of a long systems thread.
