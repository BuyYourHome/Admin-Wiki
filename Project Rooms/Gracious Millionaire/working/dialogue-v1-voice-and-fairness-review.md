# Dialogue v1 Voice And Fairness Review

## Findings

- Jenny is primary in her own sourced chapters and is not used only as a critic of Wes.
- Wes remains primary in Wes-authored source chapters, with Jenny asking caution, family-peace, and unresolved-learning questions where her perspective is relevant but not over-invented.
- Contextual sources such as Bill Johnson notes, Providence Landing inventories, Rosebrooks file inventory, and Romans 4 are handled mostly through narration or questions rather than being placed in either person's mouth as direct memory.
- The manuscript leaves unresolved material unresolved, especially the Orphaned Property, Providence Landing, and places where one spouse has not supplied a matching source response.
