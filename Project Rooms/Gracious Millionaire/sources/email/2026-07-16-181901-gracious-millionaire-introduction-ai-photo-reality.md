## Body

This is something to add to the introduction.

I expect any author will have the urge to portray themselves in the best light. That tendency may be enhanced when using AI to edit manuscripts. An interesting example of this came up when I submitted a photo to Jean to use for the cover of our book. She enhanced the photo and the people in it looked like her and I . The dress and posture were familia. Everything looked good until you looked closely at the faces and saw strangers.

It isn't good enough to tell a nice story that does seem real when you look closely at the details. Those faces were of nice looking people, they just were not us. (I'll provide photos to use as examples later.)

I had to keep going back to the drawing board to get an illustration that both looked nice and resembled reality. We have to take the same cautions when telling our story. It would be to easy to tell a good story and gloss over the details to make us look good. That isn't what this book is about.
