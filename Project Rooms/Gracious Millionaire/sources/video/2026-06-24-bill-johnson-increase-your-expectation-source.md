## Description From YouTube Metadata

Bill Johnson teaches on God’s reward system and encourages us to increase our expectations for what God wants to do. As we approach a new year, let us consider what God has put in our hand and dream about how He would increase it. God’s Father heart towards us is good and He longs to bless us and increase everything that we are stewarding. Hebrews 11: 6 teaches that God rewards those who diligently seek Him, and the parable of the talents reveals the increase that God wants for us. Whatever we are stewarding, whether it be faith, our prayer life, relationships, resources, spiritual revelation, business, or other, let’s dream and continue to lean into God for growth and increase. When we truly believe that God is good we will live with confidence and purpose as we seek Him and walk in greater power and authority in the Holy Spirit.
