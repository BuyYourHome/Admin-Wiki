# CFPB Regulation Z Section 1026.36 Source Note

## Source

- Title: 12 CFR 1026.36, Prohibited acts or practices and certain requirements for credit secured by a dwelling
- Publisher: Consumer Financial Protection Bureau, Interactive Regulation Z
- URL: https://www.consumerfinance.gov/rules-policy/regulations/1026/36/
- Captured: 2026-06-05

## Status

Authoritative federal regulatory source for seller-financer exclusions and loan-originator-related requirements under Regulation Z.

## Key Points For This Project

- The one-property seller-financer exclusion is not simply "one house per year." It applies to a natural person, estate, or trust that provides seller financing for only one property in any 12-month period, with additional conditions.
- The financing must have a repayment schedule that does not result in negative amortization.
- Adjustable-rate terms have separate limitations and should be avoided unless counsel reviews them.
- If Buy Your Home or the actual seller is an LLC or other entity, counsel must confirm whether the one-property exclusion applies or whether a different seller-financer rule applies.

## Use In Drafting

Use this source to create the transaction-screening checklist before any buyer creditworthiness review begins. Do not draft the SOP as if the one-property exclusion automatically applies.
