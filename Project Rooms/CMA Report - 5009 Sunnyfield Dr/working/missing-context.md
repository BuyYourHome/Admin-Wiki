# Missing Context

- Year built / HUD label information.
- Manufacturer/model and dimensions.
- Square footage.
- Bedroom and bathroom count.
- Interior and exterior condition.
- Roof, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, skirting, tie-down, and foundation status.
- Title status and lien status.
- Monthly lot rent and what it includes.
- Park/community transfer requirements and whether the home may stay in place.
- Whether any repairs or upgrades have been completed recently.
