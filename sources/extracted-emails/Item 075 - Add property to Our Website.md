Adding property to Website. 

This will show up from the Properties Menu of the BuyYourHomeLLC.com web site.

1.	Go to https://my.reiblackbook.com/In
2.	In the Deals drop down menu at the top choose Property Pipeline
3.	Search for address you want to add to website
4.	Click on the marketing tab at the far right above the Orange line
5.	Click on Property Details to the far left under the orange line
6.	Set Publish to on under Website Status
7.	Set Publish to on under Featured Website Status
8.	Scroll down to Property Basics and edit the number of beds., baths and the square feet. Click save.
9.	At the very top of the page on the right side next to the property address choose Under Contract from the Market Status drop down menu. This adds the property to the private lenders page on the website.
10.	Scroll down to the Property Description section and edit with the text below-

i. Catchy Title: Available for Private Lender Funds

ii. Short Description: Available to accept $XXX,XXX.00 from Private Lenders

11.	Save property picture from Zillow- right click picture, choose ‘save as’ and save to computer
12.	Scroll down in REI to Property Images and upload the picture you just saved from Zillow

When Property is Sold

This will show up from the Properties Menu of the BuyYourHomeLLC.com web site. It will have a SOLD label displayed and will show up after the available properties

This will be removed from the Investors Menu of the BuyYourHomeLLC.com web site.

*	Go to https://my.reiblackbook.com/

	*	Follow steps 1-5 from above to pull property up
	*	Choose sold from the Market Status drop down menu at the top of the page on the right.
	*	Scroll down to Property Details and edit with text below

Catchy Title: SOLD

Short Description: This property was another Buy Your Home success!
