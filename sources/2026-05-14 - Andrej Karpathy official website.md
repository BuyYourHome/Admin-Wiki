## Raw Notes

Karpathy's website describes him as an AI researcher and educator. The timeline lists a 2024 onward period focused on educational AI videos; a 2023-2024 return to OpenAI to build a team around midtraining and synthetic data generation; a 2017-2022 role as Director of AI at Tesla leading the Autopilot computer vision team; a 2015-2017 role as a research scientist and founding member at OpenAI; PhD work at Stanford with Fei-Fei Li; an MSc at the University of British Columbia; and a BSc at the University of Toronto.

The site highlights teaching work including Stanford CS231n and YouTube lectures. It also links to writing such as "Software 2.0", "A Recipe for Training Neural Networks", and "The Unreasonable Effectiveness of Recurrent Neural Networks", plus projects such as micrograd, char-rnn, arxiv-sanity, neuraltalk2, ConvNetJS, and others.

## Key Claims

- Karpathy is an AI researcher and educator.
- He was a founding member of OpenAI and later Director of AI at Tesla.
- At Tesla he led computer vision work for Autopilot.
- He designed and was primary instructor for Stanford's first deep learning class, CS231n.
- His current public work emphasizes AI education videos and practical explanations of LLMs.
