# Andrej Karpathy

## Summary

Andrej Karpathy is an AI researcher and educator known for deep learning education, computer vision work, OpenAI, Tesla Autopilot, and practical code-first explanations of neural networks and LLMs.

## Career Timeline

- 2005-2009: BSc at the University of Toronto, double majoring in computer science and physics with a math minor.
- 2009-2011: MSc at the University of British Columbia, working on learning controllers for simulated physical figures.
- 2011-2015: PhD at Stanford with Fei-Fei Li, focused on convolutional and recurrent neural networks for computer vision and language.
- 2015-2017: Research scientist and founding member at OpenAI.
- 2017-2022: Director of AI at Tesla, leading computer vision work for Autopilot.
- 2023-2024: Returned to OpenAI and built a team focused on midtraining and synthetic data generation.
- 2024 onward: Public focus on AI education videos and AI-native education through [[Eureka Labs]].

## Why He Matters

- He helped make deep learning more legible through CS231n, blog posts, and code-first courses.
- His projects often function as executable explanations: small enough to read, realistic enough to teach useful engineering habits.
- His public framing of concepts such as Software 2.0, vibe coding, and the [[Karpathy LLM Wiki Pattern]] has influenced how developers think about AI-assisted work.
