# SOP - Item 025: Respond to Remove Request in REI

## Purpose

Find contacts who requested removal and mark them for deletion in REI Blackbook.

## When To Use

Use this when REI alerts, texts, or emails indicate someone wants to be removed from contact lists.

## Owner

- Role: Lead Sourcing / Office Assistant
- Backup: Wes

## Category

Lead Sourcing

## Inputs Needed

- REI Blackbook access
- New alert notifications
- Contact name or alert record

## Tools / Systems

- REI Blackbook

## Steps

1. Open REI Blackbook Contacts.
2. Click the notification bell in the upper-right area.
3. Review each alert icon/category in the notification dropdown.
4. Look for text or email requests to be removed from the list.
5. Open each relevant alert.
6. Click the contact name at the top of the alert.
7. Edit the contact profile.
8. In the set-to-delete section, mark the contact for deletion.
9. Save the profile.
10. Repeat until all removal requests are processed.

## Decision Rules

- Treat removal requests as high priority.
- Do not keep texting or emailing a contact after a removal request is identified.
- If the request is ambiguous, add it to Questions for Review before further outreach.

## Common Mistakes

- Clearing the alert without updating the contact profile.
- Marking only one contact when multiple related contacts requested removal.
