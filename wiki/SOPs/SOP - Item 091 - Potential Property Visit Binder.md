# SOP - Item 091: Potential Property Visit Binder

## Purpose

Prepare route information, contact details, QR codes, and binder materials for Wes to visit potential foreclosure properties.

## When To Use

Use this after returned mail and foreclosure status review identifies properties worth visiting.

## Owner

- Role: Lead Sourcing / Office Assistant
- Backup: Wes

## Category

Lead Sourcing

## Inputs Needed

- Zone or sale list
- REI contact records
- Property addresses
- Returned envelopes and contact sheets
- Property Visit Zone Sheet template

## Tools / Systems

- REI Blackbook
- Google Maps
- Canva QR Code Generator
- Potential Property Visit Binder
- Email

## Steps

1. Filter contacts by zone or sale list.
2. Review the properties in the filtered list.
3. Open Google Maps.
4. Add each property address as a stop.
5. Set the starting location as `Your Location`.
6. Reorder the stops to create the easiest route.
7. Limit route packages to about five properties when practical.
8. Start a new email to Wes with the subject `Map to [Zone X or Sale Properties]`.
9. In the email body, list each address in route order.
10. Under each address, include the REI contact URL.
11. Add spacing between property groups.
12. Copy the Google Maps route URL and place it above the address list.
13. Open or create a note in each REI contact.
14. Replace the prior note content with the complete route list from the email.
15. Send the email to Wes.
16. Create or update the Property Visit Zone Sheet.
17. Print REI contact sheets for each contact, normally pages 1 and 2, double-sided.
18. Add contact sheets and returned envelopes to the binder.
19. Separate groups with a sticky note.
20. Generate a QR code for the Google Maps route using the approved Canva account.
21. Download the QR code.
22. Add the QR code to the Property Visit Zone Sheet.
23. Print the updated sheet and add it to the binder.
24. Leave the binder in the agreed location for Wes.
25. Check the binder daily for visited-property notes.
26. After visits, review Wes's notes for updated information, new contacts, added notes, or files to close.
27. Update REI and the ForeclosureList as directed.
28. Add the visited tag to contacts that have been visited.
29. Move contact sheets to the visited section of the binder.

## Decision Rules

- Group properties into manageable route packages, around five properties when possible.
- Do not discard returned envelopes until the visit review process is complete.
- Use approved credentials for Canva; do not store passwords in the SOP.

## Common Mistakes

- Sending addresses without REI contact URLs.
- Forgetting to save the route URL or QR code.
- Not updating REI after Wes leaves notes.
