# SOP - Item 104: Import Transactions into QuickBooks

## Purpose

Review and confirm bank transactions in QuickBooks so accounting records stay accurate.

## When To Use

Use this when bank or credit card transactions need to be reviewed/imported in QuickBooks.

## Owner

- Role: Accounting / Office Assistant
- Backup: Wes or Jenny

## Category

Accounting

## Inputs Needed

- Company to review, such as BYH or SYH
- QuickBooks access
- Online banking access if transaction details need confirmation
- Receipts or supporting documents

## Tools / Systems

- QuickBooks
- Online banking
- Receipt records

## Steps

1. Open QuickBooks.
2. Choose the company that needs transaction review.
3. Open Bank Accounts.
4. Review all pending or imported transactions.
5. For each transaction, compare the amount, date, vendor, and description to available receipts or bank records.
6. If more detail is needed, open online banking and verify the transaction.
7. Confirm or update the transaction description.
8. Assign the correct account, category, class, and property when applicable.
9. Save or confirm the transaction.
10. Continue until all current transactions are reviewed.
11. Flag unclear transactions for Wes or Jenny before guessing.

## Decision Rules

- Do not confirm a transaction if the vendor, class, or property is unclear.
- Use online banking only to verify details, not to change accounting records unless directed.
- Property-related transactions need the correct property class.

## Common Mistakes

- Confirming transactions with generic descriptions.
- Assigning the wrong company or property class.
- Ignoring transactions that need follow-up.
