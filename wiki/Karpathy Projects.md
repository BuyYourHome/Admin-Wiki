# Karpathy Projects

## Summary

Karpathy's projects often double as teaching artifacts: they are compact, readable implementations that make a technical idea concrete. This page tracks projects that are useful for learning, reference, or understanding his technical style.

## Project Map

### micrograd

Tiny scalar-valued autograd engine with a small neural network library. Best treated as a readable path into backpropagation and automatic differentiation.

### makemore

Character-level language modeling project used in the Zero to Hero sequence. Best treated as a bridge from simple language models to neural architectures.

### nanoGPT

nanoGPT is a compact repository for training and finetuning GPT-style models. Its README describes it as a rewrite of minGPT that emphasizes practical training. As of the README's November 2025 update, nanoGPT is deprecated in favor of nanochat but remains useful as a historical and educational reference.

### minGPT

Earlier minimal GPT implementation. Useful for education-first reading and for understanding how nanoGPT evolved.

### char-rnn

Older character-level recurrent neural network project connected to Karpathy's writing on recurrent neural networks.

### arxiv-sanity

Research discovery tool for making arXiv papers easier to search, sort, and browse.

### ConvNetJS

Browser-based deep learning library written in JavaScript, useful as an example of small, inspectable ML tooling.
