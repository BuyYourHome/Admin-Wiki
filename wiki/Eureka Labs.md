# Eureka Labs

## Summary

Eureka Labs is an AI-native education company associated with Andrej Karpathy. Its stated model is teacher-designed course material supported by an AI teaching assistant, with LLM101n as the first announced product.

## Model

Eureka Labs frames education as a teacher plus AI assistant system:

- A human expert designs the course materials.
- An AI teaching assistant guides students through the materials.
- The goal is to increase both reach and depth of education.

## LLM101n

LLM101n is described as an undergraduate-level AI course where students train their own AI. It is intended to be both an online course material set and, according to the announcement, a basis for digital and physical cohorts.
