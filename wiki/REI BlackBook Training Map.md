## Priority Learning Areas For Jean Wright

### 1. Profit Dial / Texting

Status: Started.

Important learned points:

- Profit Dial is the phone/text/RVM system inside REI BlackBook.
- Text replies are handled from Profit Dial Inbox > Texts.
- To reply to a text, open the text message, choose the correct Profit Dial number, type the message, and click the envelope/send icon.
- Individual texts can also be sent from a contact list row or from a contact record's Actions menu.
- Contacts must be opted in before text messages can be sent.

### 2. A2P / SMS Compliance

Status: Started.

Important learned points:

- REI BlackBook separates A2P registration and texting setup into its own support category.
- SMS compliance involves legal rules and carrier/industry rules.
- The REI guide says businesses should assume promotional text messaging requires express written consent.
- Conversational messages initiated by a consumer are treated differently from promotional broadcasts, but caution is still required.
- Opt-out instructions, message frequency, business identity, terms/privacy links, and message/data rate disclosures are key compliance elements.

Source articles:
