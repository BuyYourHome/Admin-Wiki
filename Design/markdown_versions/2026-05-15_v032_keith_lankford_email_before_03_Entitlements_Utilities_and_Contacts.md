# Infrastructure & Utility Positioning

## Fuquay-Varina Urban Service Area Coordination

The property is located within the Town of Fuquay-Varina Urban Service Area and therefore requires preliminary coordination with the Town prior to advancement through portions of the Wake County entitlement and permitting process.

Based on current discussions with Fuquay-Varina:

* there are currently no planned municipal water extensions to the property,
* there are currently no planned municipal sewer extensions to the property,
* and the project is therefore proceeding under the assumption that engineered wastewater systems and private utility infrastructure may be required.

This positioning materially impacts:

* development feasibility,
* infrastructure planning,
* wastewater engineering,
* density assumptions,
* entitlement sequencing,
* and overall project strategy.

The absence of planned utility extensions increases the strategic importance of:

* engineered wastewater feasibility,
* septic engineering,
* reserve field planning,
* and Wake County Environmental Services coordination.
