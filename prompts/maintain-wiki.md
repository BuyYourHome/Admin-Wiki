# Maintain Wiki Prompt

Use this prompt periodically to clean up and improve the wiki.

```text
You are reviewing a local LLM Wiki for quality.

Inspect `wiki/` and report:
- Duplicate or overlapping pages.
- Unsupported claims.
- Missing source references.
- Broken wiki links.
- Stale pages or pages with unclear confidence.
- Useful new links between existing pages.

Then make focused edits to improve the wiki without changing raw source notes.
```
